Lake Shadroe Resort & Marina · Pricing & Market Context

Why There Are No Comps in Cape Coral

Lake Shadroe is the first new-build condo-hotel in Cape Coral. There is no comparable product in this submarket. The independent appraisal had to pull comps from Sarasota, Naples, and Bonita Springs — and adjusted them down for Cape Coral's location.

Understanding the Appraisal

BBG's independent MAI appraisal valued completed Lake Shadroe units at $1,565/SF (2BR) and $1,770/SF (3BR). These figures are not what Cape Coral resales trade at — they represent what this product class trades at across Southwest Florida, after BBG applied negative 2.5%–5% location adjustments to every comparable because Sarasota and Naples are considered superior locations. Even after those downward adjustments, the appraised values significantly exceed our Founder's pricing of $925–$985/SF.

Why the Comps Come From Outside Cape Coral

Zero New-Build Condo-Hotel Inventory in Cape Coral

There is no new-construction condo-hotel product in Cape Coral. Period. The only comparable waterfront condo product is Cape Harbour Marina Village, which trades at $1,050/SF — but Cape Harbour is a residential condo with HOA-governed rental rights, no professional operator, and no resort amenities. It is not a condo-hotel. BBG correctly identified that the comparable set for Lake Shadroe must come from analogous product types in adjacent SWFL markets.

BBG Adjusted Down for Location

Every comp in the appraisal — Sarasota, Naples, Bonita Springs — received a negative 2.5%–5% location adjustment because BBG considers those submarkets superior to Cape Coral. This is built into the $1,565–$1,770 reconciled values. The appraisal is not saying Cape Coral commands Naples pricing — it's saying Lake Shadroe's product class commands this pricing even after discounting for Cape Coral's location.

BBG Adjusted Up for Amenities and STR Rights

BBG applied +20–30% amenity adjustments to comps because none of them offer what Lake Shadroe delivers: an integrated marina with boat slips, a Dalal wellness club, a waterfront restaurant, an infinity pool with beach, and — critically — commercially zoned nightly rental rights that no HOA or municipality can restrict. These are structural advantages that residential condo comps simply do not have.

Launch Pricing Is Conservative to Both the Appraisal and the Market

At $925/SF Founder's pricing, Lake Shadroe is priced 41% below the BBG appraisal, 26% below the SWFL new-construction comp average of $1,246/SF, and 12% below Cape Harbour at $1,050/SF — the closest Cape Coral comp, which is a materially inferior product. The blended sellout at ~$1,150/SF across all 48 units aligns almost exactly with the BBG as-complete value of $81.68M.
